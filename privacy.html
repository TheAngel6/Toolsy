Privacy Policy for Mr. Toolsy

Effective Date: 15/07/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Mr. Toolsy ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information. Your privacy and data security are extremely important to us.

The Core Principle: We Don't Store Your Data. Our philosophy is to collect the absolute minimum data required for the Bot to function. We do not store your embed content, command history, or any personal information in a persistent database.

1. Information We Collect

To operate, the Bot temporarily processes certain information provided by you or by Discord's API.

A. Information You Directly Provide:

  • Embed Content: When you use /custom_embed, /edit_embed, or /send_webhook, the content you provide is processed in-memory to generate the final message and is immediately discarded.
  • Message & Webhook URLs: The URLs you provide for commands are used only to execute that single command and are then discarded.

B. Information We Automatically Receive from Discord:

  • User, Server, and Channel IDs: Discord sends us IDs to know who to respond to and where to send a message. This data is not stored.
  • Usernames & Avatars: When creating a footer for an embed, we use the username and avatar URL provided by Discord for that specific interaction. This is not stored.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we temporarily process is used exclusively to generate and send messages as you command, respond to you, attribute content where applicable, and ensure compliance with our Terms of Service.

3. Data Storage, Retention, and Deletion

Mr. Toolsy is a "stateless" bot. This means we do not have a database for storing user-generated content. All data related to a command is processed in-memory and is cleared as soon as the command is completed. There is no user data for us to retain or delete because we do not store it.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties. The only "sharing" that occurs is the necessary transmission of your user-generated content to Discord's API so that it can be displayed in a channel or sent to a webhook.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not store your data, the primary way to exercise your rights is by managing the content you've created within Discord itself. You have the right to delete any message sent by the bot (with proper permissions) and edit them using the /edit_embed command.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at toolsymr@gmail.com or join our support server: https://discord.gg/RcMS9jevKD.
